Mysore, which means ‘city of palaces,’ is a beautiful city in southern India. Mysore, the palace city of India

, was the capital of the Kingdom of Mysore for over two centuries and is home to many magnificent

structures like the Temple of Shiva. The city also has several great parks and lakes. You can explore it all

at your leisure while staying in one or more excellent hotels or apartments in Mysore. It is located in the

southern part of Karnataka. Mysore is at a distance of 602 and 496 kilometres from Bangalore and Mangalore,

respectively.

guinness world record sea shell museum

 | 

The Guinness World Records have recognized the museum for having the largest collection of sea shells used in art. Visitors to the museum can also learn about the different types of sea shells, their scientific names, and their habitats. The museum also features a gift shop where visitors can purchase souvenirs from sea shells.

The museum is a popular tourist attraction in Mahabalipuram and is visited by thousands yearly. Nearby places include the famous Shore Temple, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, and the Pancha Rathas, a group of five monolithic temples in Mahabalipuram. The town is also known for its beautiful beaches and popular destination for water sports enthusiasts.
